How Does OpenClaw Work?

At its core, OpenClaw is a gateway that connects your messaging apps to AI models. Here's the simple flow:

  1. You send a message via Telegram, WhatsApp, or web interface
  2. OpenClaw receives it and checks your agent's memory and instructions
  3. Your agent decides what to do â€” answer directly, browse the web, or use a tool
  4. The response comes back through your messaging app
  5. Everything is saved to your agent's memory for future context

The magic happens in your agent's personality files â€” simple text documents that define who your agent is, how it communicates, and what it should remember about you.
