Master the mindset shift from asking to delegating. Learn the 3-part formula for getting the best results.

üéØ Asking vs Delegating

The biggest mistake new users make? Treating their agent like a search engine. Here's the difference:

‚ùå Asking (LLM Style)

"What are good Italian restaurants near me?"

‚úÖ Delegating (Agent Style)

"Find 3 highly-rated Italian restaurants within 10 minutes. Check availability Saturday 7pm. Book the best one."

The 3-Part Delegation Formula

1. What
The task you want done
2. Context
Background information
3. Success Criteria
How you want the result
üí°

Why This Matters

Agents are action-oriented. When you ask "what is..." they answer. When you delegate "find and book..." they execute. The more you delegate, the more value you get.
